Drinks on cruises

The most popular question when planning a cruise ship vacation is cruise drinks. What's included and what's not. Officially, cruise lines designate the type of food as all inclusive. But "All Inclusive" on a cruise is different from a Turkish one. On any cruise line, the minimum type of food is full board: breakfast, lunch and dinner. You can eat both on the Swedish line and in the a la carte restaurant. And at least breakfast included tea, coffee, water on a buffet. At other meals, the minimum is water on the buffet. If you order any of the drinks in the restaurant à la carte, then they will go for a surcharge.
